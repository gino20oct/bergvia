Investing with Purpose and Perspective

Bergvia Family Office invests across asset classes — real estate, listed equities, fixed income and private equity — with the objective of creating enduring value and intergenerational wealth.

We invest both directly and through partnerships with leading asset managers across sectors including:

  • Private Equity and Venture Capital
  • Infrastructure and Real Assets
  • Technology and Digital Innovation
  • Real Estate Development and Management

In addition, Bergvia acquires and develops operating businesses with strong growth potential, principally in the fields of:

  • Circular Economy
  • Beauty & Cosmetics
  • Health Tech
  • Technology and Digital Services

Our investment activity combines strategic patience with entrepreneurial agility — building value steadily, one decision at a time.
