A Family Perspective on Long-Term Value

Founded and managed by our family, Bergvia reflects a cross-generational vision rooted in the Swiss and Italian Alps, a place where stability, resilience and foresight are part of the landscape.

We invest our own capital to preserve and grow the family’s assets while supporting innovation and responsible progress.

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy is guided by three principles:

  • Long-term orientation — patient capital focused on sustainable value creation.
  • Entrepreneurial mindset — active ownership and hands-on partnership with founders and managers.
  • Responsible growth — contributing to the communities and environment in which we operate.
